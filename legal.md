# VEYL PRIVACY POLICY AND TERMS OF SERVICE

**Effective Date:** April 25, 2026

**Operator:** Glyphteck Corp

**Contact:** `links.contact`

**Websites:** `links.root`, `links.veyl`

This document contains both the Privacy Policy and the Terms of Service for veyl.

veyl is a direct-transaction Bitcoin product built by Glyphteck Corp. It combines passkey authentication, a locally unlocked encrypted vault, a non-custodial wallet, and end-to-end encrypted chat.

veyl is intentionally designed so that Glyphteck Corp is structurally separated from the most sensitive user secrets and user-controlled content. Except where this document expressly says otherwise, Glyphteck Corp does not have your vault password, decrypted seed, private keys, or plaintext private message content during normal operation of the product.

## PART I. PRIVACY POLICY

### 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Glyphteck Corp handles information in connection with veyl, including the iOS app, related web properties, and supporting infrastructure.

This Privacy Policy applies to service data that Glyphteck Corp processes in connection with operating veyl. It does not change the public nature of blockchain data or the policies of third-party providers and platforms that veyl depends on.

### 2. Core Design Principle

veyl is built to minimize the amount of sensitive user data that Glyphteck Corp can access.

In particular:

- Glyphteck Corp does **not** know your vault password.
- Glyphteck Corp does **not** control your private keys.
- Glyphteck Corp does **not** custody your funds.
- Glyphteck Corp does **not** normally possess plaintext end-to-end encrypted chat messages.
- Glyphteck Corp does **not** have a technical recovery path for lost wallet secrets that only exist on your device.

This separation is a product design goal, but it also means support is limited. If you lose device-controlled secrets, Glyphteck Corp may be unable to help recover them.

### 3. Information Glyphteck Corp Processes

veyl processes limited categories of information to operate the service.

Glyphteck Corp may receive information:

- directly from you when you register, choose a username, upload an avatar, use wallet or chat features, configure settings, contact support, or submit a report
- from your device and operating system when you enable notifications, use camera or photo features, use passkeys, or opt into local biometric unlock features
- from service infrastructure and providers involved in authentication, storage, notifications, wallet connectivity, and product operations

#### A. Account and profile information

Depending on how you use veyl, Glyphteck Corp may process:

- account identifiers
- passkey-related credential records
- username
- avatar
- wallet public key
- chat public key
- presence state
- user settings

#### B. Service-side encrypted data

Glyphteck Corp may store or process:

- encrypted seed blobs
- encrypted chat payloads
- encrypted attachment payloads
- encrypted or packed message metadata needed for delivery and sync

These records may be stored on service infrastructure, but that does **not** mean Glyphteck Corp can read them in plaintext.

#### C. Operational and device-related service data

Glyphteck Corp may process:

- push tokens
- app settings
- device or session identifiers required for notifications or service sync
- logs needed for uptime, debugging, abuse prevention, and security

#### D. Device permissions and local device features

Depending on which features you choose to use, veyl may request or use:

- camera access to scan QR codes and capture images you choose to send
- photo library access to select images or files you choose to send, or to save media to your device when you request it
- notification permissions to deliver chat and service alerts
- local biometric unlock features such as Face ID to unlock app data stored on your device

Apple and your device operating system handle passkeys and biometric templates. Glyphteck Corp does not receive your raw biometric template.

#### E. Public or user-visible information

Some information is designed to be visible to other authenticated users or counterparties, including:

- username
- avatar
- wallet public key
- chat public key
- online or active state

This public or counterparty-visible information is part of how veyl resolves users, routes payments, and opens chats.

#### F. Blockchain and network data

When you use wallet functionality, veyl necessarily interacts with Bitcoin-related services and public blockchain data. On-chain information is public by nature, may be permanently visible to others, and cannot be deleted or made private by Glyphteck Corp after broadcast.

### 4. Information Glyphteck Corp Does Not Normally Have

During normal operation of the service, Glyphteck Corp does not have:

- your vault password
- your decrypted seed
- your wallet private keys
- your chat private keys
- your plaintext end-to-end encrypted message content

Glyphteck Corp also cannot:

- reverse completed blockchain transactions
- recover lost private keys
- recover lost passwords that only exist on your device
